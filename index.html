Welcome

Islamic Community of Bay Area Bosniaks is an Religion organization which covers the Bay Area Bosniaks members. Our organization was founded in 1997. It was registered as a non profit organozation in the year of 2003. From 2003 all the way until January 2010 ICBAB did not own its own mosque, instead we were renting.

In the year of 2010 we were able to purchase our own home to handle of duties. Our organization has over 370 families and we are a part of ICNAB (Islamic Community of North America Bosniaks).

In our organization we have daily prayers, Jummuah, sunday school, Quran practise and youth get togethers. Our community board are the ones who organize and are preparing the younger generation to hopefully one day hand over the duties.
